While enrolled in an interactive design course at the University of Minnesota, I completed an analysis and redesign for a local nonprofit to aid their current and potential members online.
After meeting with the client and conducting a heuristic analysis of the existing site, user testing in a usability lab was done as a class to inform our design goals and process.
Using a logo by a fellow classmate, I created a new web presence for the group with an emphasis on usability, attracting new members, and increasing attendance at workshops.
