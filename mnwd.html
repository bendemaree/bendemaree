Now defunct, Minnesota Web Development was a student group at the University of Minneosta that I participated in as an officer. I also designed an identity for the group.
While active, the group made use of the University of Minnesota's GitHub instance for sharing code and assets. The site continues to be maintained on Amazon Web Services.
The mark reflects our Minneosta origins while echoing our principles of clean design and development and was designed to be iterated by future members.
